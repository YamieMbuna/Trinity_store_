Payment FAQs

For customers in Malawi, we accept Airtel Money and Mpamba mobile payments, as well as Visa credit/debit cards issued by Malawian banks.

All prices are displayed in USD. For Malawian customers, your payment will be automatically converted to MWK at the current exchange rate when you pay with Airtel Money or Mpamba. The exact rate will be shown before you confirm payment.

Trinity Store doesn't charge any additional fees for payments. However, your bank or mobile money provider may apply standard transaction fees according to their policies.

We use industry-standard 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions. We don't store your credit card details on our servers. Mobile money payments are processed through secure gateways provided by Airtel and TNM.
